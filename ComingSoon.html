20ft Single Apartment 2



The 6m Humble Abode

Welcome to the 6m Humble Abode. This stylish, off-grid, 20ft shipping container is built from a high-cube shipping container for added head room.

The apartment has a minimalistic design language that focuses on creating a spacious living environment, with a focus on sustainability and functionality. The furniture is carefully chosen to be dual purpose where necessary, putting your needs as a student first. The room comes with the following amenities:

  • Kitchenette
  • Dining/Study table
  • Double Bed
  • Toilet with shower
  • Wi-Fi
